Security Checklists

Here at computing cafe we hold online and cyber security as the highest priority and one of the largest modern threats to most people. This is why we have devised a few security checklists for different levels of computer skill and interest. This way whoever you may be, you can still keep yourself safe online. To access these, simply click the button that relates to your skill category and start reading. For a more in depth look or if you need help with any of the items on the list. Any of the staff in our discord server will be able to help and feel free to tweet me (@Alfreaca) and I will try and help as soon as I can.
